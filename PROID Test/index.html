Our Pillars of Excellence

Discover the core values that define the NP experience.

Forging the Future

From our earliest days, NP has been a hub for innovation. We were pioneers in introducing new diploma programs and integrating technology into our curriculum. Today, our state-of-the-art labs, industry collaborations, and focus on design thinking prepare students to be future-ready leaders and problem-solvers in a rapidly changing world.

A Vibrant Community

Life at NP has always been more than just academics. The vibrant history of our Co-Curricular Activities (CCAs), from sports and arts to special interest groups, has shaped a holistic and dynamic campus culture. These experiences foster teamwork, leadership, and lifelong friendships, forming the heart of the NP memory.

Service with a Heart

Rooted in our founding mission, NP has a long-standing tradition of giving back to the community. Through local and overseas service-learning projects, our students and staff have consistently made a positive impact, embodying the values of empathy, social responsibility, and active citizenship that define the NP spirit.

1963: The Dawn of an Era

On this historic year, Ngee Ann College was established by the Ngee Ann Kongsi with a vision to provide quality education. It started with just over 100 students at the Teochew Building on Tank Road, laying the foundation for what would become a premier polytechnic in Singapore.

×

1982: A New Identity as Ngee Ann Polytechnic

A landmark year where Ngee Ann Technical College was officially renamed Ngee Ann Polytechnic. This change reflected its expanded role and curriculum, aligning it with Singapore's growing need for a skilled technical workforce and marking its evolution into a full-fledged polytechnic.

×

The 2000s: Innovation and Expansion

The turn of the millennium marked a period of rapid growth. NP embraced new technologies, launched interdisciplinary programs like the NP-NUS Overseas Colleges programme, and expanded its campus facilities. This era cemented NP's reputation as a forward-thinking institution committed to nurturing global-ready graduates.
